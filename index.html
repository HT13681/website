The members of the Birla Cyber Security Club and The Coping Centre joined hands to form the Hidden Tears initiative.We decided to do a collaboration project together because our objectives were similar- raise awareness about the harmful effects of bullying and to promote kindness, empathy, and respect. Over the multiple months we worked together, we have developed a bond of friendship which enabled us to do work better together. If a member was unable to do a particular task other members readily covered up for them. With teamwork and cooperation, we were able to achieve our targets
Our club aims to achieve two primary objectives: firstly, to increase awareness about the negative impacts of bullying and promote anti-bullying messages through various activities and events. Secondly, we strive to support and provide resources for individuals who have been affected by bullying, while also working towards creating a safe environment where everyone can feel comfortable sharing their personal stories and experiences.
We hope to create an era across Qatar, and eventually the world in which one lives in peace and harmony with the other.

How do we go about these issues?

Implementation

To achieve their objectives, Hidden Tears conducted several activities, including anti-bullying campaigns, talks in classrooms, and posters designed to educate and inform students about the dangers of bullying. They also utilized social media platforms to reach a wider audience and promote their anti-bullying campaign. In addition, Hidden Tears implemented several initiatives to offer support and resources to individuals who have experienced bullying, including a peer mentoring program, a website, and an AI chatbot. Through their efforts, Hidden Tears aims to create a safe and inclusive environment where everyone can feel comfortable sharing their personal stories and experiences while promoting a more empathetic and kind community.

Live Podcast

In this podcast, we will hear from real victims of cybercrime and sexual assault who will share their stories of how they were targeted, what happened to them, and how they have coped with the aftermath. We will explore the impact that these crimes have had on their lives and discuss ways to prevent and address these issues. Public Interviews

In this interview series, we will travel around to different locations in an around Qatar --detailed locations will be updated soon-- to speak with individuals about their experiences with bullying. After each interview, we will give the participant a piece of chocolate as a symbol of kindness and support. Through these conversations, we hope to raise awareness about the impact of bullying and promote a culture of kindness and empathy. By combining meaningful discussions with a small act of kindness, we hope to spread positivity and make a difference in the lives of those who have been affected by bullying.

Become A Volunteer

Let's make a difference in the lives of others

Volunteers are an essential part of our organization and we greatly appreciate those who are willing to give their time and energy to support our cause. There are many ways to get involved, from hosting events in auditoriums to helping give out presents and chocolates to children in need. If you have a passion for event planning and organization, consider hosting an event in one of our auditoriums to help us spread awareness and raise funds for our programs. If you enjoy working with children, we would love your help in giving out presents and chocolates during our holiday events. Additionally, we are always in need of volunteers who can respond to emails and help connect individuals in need to the right authorities. No matter how you choose to volunteer, your efforts will make a meaningful difference in the lives of those we serve.
